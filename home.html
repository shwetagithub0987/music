ABOUT ME

I'm a Delhi based Musician. I currently performs solo sets covering vocals and guitar.

My genre of music is sufi, retro & bollywood. I have been learning Hindustani classical music from last six years, so I also loves performing semi-classical tracks. I have started my music career in Dehradun 2 years ago. It's been more than a year since I have shifted to Delhi. Currently performing at various venues among Delhi and out of Delhi. I have also done many private parties like house parties, anniversaries, birthday parties, corporate events etc.
